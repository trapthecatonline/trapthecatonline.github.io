Would you like to challenge your intelligence and tactical skills? Then welcome to Trap the Cat, an exciting puzzle game, where you need to block a curious black cat in the maze using intricate schemes and strategic tricks. This little furry creature is extremely smart, so you will need to think wisely before attempting the next movement!

How to Play Trap the Cat

Trap the Cat doesnt include too many features, but it remains refreshing each time you start the game. Every session in this game is unique as you already have some blocks on the field coloured, which offers different variants of solution. Keep in mind several rules and try to outsmart your animal opponent:

  • The cat cannot step on the colour tiles. If you click on any cell in the game, it will be automatically coloured, making the animal avoid it. This way you may capture it step by step, cutting off any way to escape.
  • You both move in turns. Although in Trap the Cat you are the one who starts the game, it doesnt mean it gives you too many advantages! The cat may easily outwit you, unless you calculate all the moves in advance.
  • There is no time limit. Luckily, the game doesnt impose any limitations in time, so you have a chance to think through your new action meticulously. Use this Trap the Cat feature to construct a tactic and ensure that the creature will be trapped.
  • The animal shouldnt leave the field. The main objective in Trap the Cat is to prevent the animal from escaping the space by any means. Watch in what direction it is about to proceed and set a tap there to top it from moving further.

Learn these easy statements and start your first game in Trap the Cat. The animal will always be in the middle of the playground, but you never know where it is going to go. Try to predict its movements and paint the blocks in the way that will securely keep it inside the space.

Develop a Tricky Plan

In Trap the Cat game one of the crucial skills is the ability to manipulate the space in the way that will flabbergast your opponent. The tiny fur ball will use any opportunity to get out of the place, so you will need to get creative in order to keep it inside. Eventually, you will need to fully surround the creature and leave it only one empty tile.

Trap the Cat game conceals many possibilities, you just need to discover them. This light-hearted puzzle may turn into a true challenge once the task becomes harder and the cat becomes smarter! Constantly enhance your logical abilities and figure out the ideal approach to this mischievous creature.

Practice and Succeed

The game needs you to constantly master your talents, as the tasks may get quite difficult when you get a minimum of initial filled tiles. Come up with your own tips on how to quickly defeat the opponent in Trap the Cat and pick up the least number of blocks.

You may start from the distant bricks and cut off all the paths to retreat or make an attempt to envision the further actions of the creature. Utilise whatever option that works best for you, but remember that your opponent learns from its mistakes, so experimenting is crucial!
